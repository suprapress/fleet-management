# PRD: Modern Company Profile (Authority Focus)

**Project Name:** PT. Intan Jaya Pratama Digital HQ
**Primary Goal:** Authority & Trust (Branding).
**Secondary Goal:** Recruitment (Talent Acquisition).
**Audience:** Mitra Bisnis, Pihak Perbankan/Leasing, Calon Karyawan.
**Vibe:** Professional, Industrial, High-Tech, "The Gold Standard".

## Structure Strategy (The "Show, Don't Just Tell" Approach)

1. **Hero Section:** Pernyataan Identitas yang Kuat (Dominance).
2. **The "Intan" Standard (Why Us):** Menjelaskan standar operasional tinggi.
3. **Technology Integration:** Pamerkan aplikasi internal Anda. Ini kuncinya. Tunjukkan bahwa armada ini dikelola dengan *Data-Driven Decision*, bukan "kira-kira".
4. **Fleet & Capacity:** Data kuantitatif (Jumlah unit, kapasitas angkut).
5. **Recruitment (Bottom):** Form simpel untuk pelamar kerja.
